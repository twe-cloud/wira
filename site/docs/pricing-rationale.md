# Pricing rationale

Why **Wira Local costs $49 one time**.

## Product truth

Wira Local is not a monthly hosted service. The buyer pays once, installs Wira on their own computer, pairs WhatsApp through Linked Devices, and owns the local setup after install.

That means:

- no monthly Wira Local fee;
- no recurring Ni Biashara hosting charge for the local install;
- the buyer remains responsible for their machine, backups, accounts, tools, and updates;
- any paid brain/provider cost is separate if the buyer chooses ChatGPT, Claude, OpenAI API, or another paid provider.

## Why $49 one time

$49 is low enough for an owner to try Wira without feeling trapped in another SaaS bill, but high enough to signal that this is a packaged local product, not a throwaway script.

The purchase covers:

- packaged installer and setup flow;
- WhatsApp pairing guidance;
- local configuration under `~/.wira`;
- owner-lock and permission-first defaults;
- a clear path into Hermes when the buyer is ready for deeper control.

## What happens after payment

1. Buyer pays once.
2. Buyer returns to the setup page.
3. Buyer downloads the installer.
4. Buyer connects the brain/provider they want to use.
5. Buyer scans the WhatsApp QR code.
6. Wira starts as the agent name in WhatsApp until the user is ready to understand the deeper Hermes runtime.

## What this is not

- Not a managed monthly local subscription.
- Not a promise that Ni Biashara operates the buyer's machine after install.
- Not inclusive of third-party ChatGPT/API/model/provider costs.
- Not Wira Business hosting.

## Wira Business stays separate

Wira Business can still have setup and monthly managed-service pricing because Ni Biashara is operating infrastructure, monitoring, launch checks, and customer-facing WhatsApp paths. Do not mix that hosted pricing into Wira Local copy.
