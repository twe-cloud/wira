# Quick start

Get your assistant live on your WhatsApp in under 10 minutes.

## 1. Sign up

From the landing page, click **Start with [Product]**. Stripe will take you through checkout — use a test card (`4242 4242 4242 4242`, any future expiry, any CVC) if you're trying it out.

## 2. Onboarding

After payment you're redirected to **/onboarding**. Five short steps:

1. **Connect** — enter the WhatsApp number you want your assistant to live on.
2. **Voice** — paste 3-5 recent replies so it learns your tone. Or skip.
3. **Contacts** — pick who can be answered. Default: a small whitelist you grow over time.
4. **Approval** — pick how trusting it is. Default: drafts everything, you approve in one tap.
5. **Confirm** — you'll get an email with the magic activation link.

## 3. Pair WhatsApp

Open the magic link on the phone whose WhatsApp will be paired. A QR code appears. On your phone:

- **WhatsApp → Settings → Linked Devices → Link a Device** → scan the QR.

That's it. The assistant is now connected.

## 4. Test it

Have a friend text your WhatsApp. You'll see the draft reply appear in your dashboard within a few seconds. Tap **Send** to approve, or edit first.

## Troubleshooting

- **QR doesn't appear?** Refresh the magic link. If still nothing, email support — your instance may not have finished provisioning yet.
- **WhatsApp says "session expired"?** Re-scan the QR from the dashboard. Multi-device sessions expire if your phone is offline for over 14 days.
- **The assistant replied to the wrong person.** Move them off your whitelist or switch to draft-only mode in Settings.
